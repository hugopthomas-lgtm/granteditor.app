Hugo Thomas
Creator of Granted

Who made this?

I'm Hugo Thomas, a French filmmaker and developer. My first feature film, Willy 1er, premiered at Cannes. My second, Juniors, screened at festivals worldwide. Both are on Netflix. Before ending up in filmmaking, I completed a Master's in Law and two bachelor's degrees simultaneously, in France and Canada.

Along the way, I started building tools for writers and creatives. Things I wished existed. Screenplay Editor was the first. Then came Granted.

Why Granted?

When you make films in France, grant writing isn't a side task. It's the job. Between the CNC, regional funds, SOFICA schemes, and European co-production programs, I've filled out more applications than I can count. Every project means a new round of proposals, budgets, artistic notes, and annexes, each with its own formatting rules and deadlines.

After years of copy-pasting the same paragraphs across dozens of proposals, reformatting budgets at 2 AM, and scrambling to merge annexes the night before a deadline, I thought: there has to be a better way. My legal background probably helped, too. I was already wired to care about structure, precision, and not missing a clause.

Granted lives inside Google Docs, the tool most teams already use. No new software to learn, no files to migrate. Just open the sidebar and start working smarter. Save your best content once, fill in project details automatically, and package everything into a clean PDF when you're done.

Who made this?

I'm Hugo Thomas, a French filmmaker and developer. My first feature film, Willy 1er, premiered at Cannes. My second, Juniors, screened at festivals worldwide. Both are on Netflix.

Along the way, I started building tools for writers and creatives — things I wished existed. Screenplay Editor was the first. Then came Granted.

Why Granted?

I watched colleagues spend weeks rewriting the same paragraphs across proposals, copy-pasting project names into 40 fields, and scrambling to merge annexes the night before deadlines. Grant writing shouldn't be this painful.

Granted lives inside Google Docs — the tool most teams already use. No new software to learn, no files to migrate. Just open the sidebar and start working smarter. Save your best content once, fill in project details automatically, and package everything into a clean PDF when you're done.
